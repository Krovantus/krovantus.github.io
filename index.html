Beer

Beer is a low-alcohol drink  obtained by alcoholic fermentation of malt wort 
(most often based on barley) using brewer's yeast,
usually with the addition of hops.
The ethyl alcohol content in most types of beer is about 3.0-6.0% vol. 
(strong contains, as a rule, from 8.5% to 14% vol.,
sometimes light beer is also distinguished, 
which contains 1-2% vol., non-alcoholic beer is separately isolated,
which is not included here), 
dry substances (mainly carbohydrates ) 7-10%, carbon dioxide 0.48-1.0%
